Business Model

SPK’s business model is same with those of “German Mittelstand” model. Mittelstand meaning Small to Medium sized business which is family owned for generations. It further has the nature of investing on developing and upgrading existing business instead of acquiring new business or investing into new business fields. The benefit is that it will ensure the better working processes and this also help reduce risk of running out of business. Due to focus being on existing business, the business tends on perform and pick up rather better and in unorthodox way.
