Other Importation Process

Due to lack of banking facilities and US’s decade long sanction, 98% of Myanmar trade goes through Singapore. Myanmar banks have ties only with selected bank and it has strongest bonds with Singapore, making Singapore most reliant partner for trade. Thus, SPK has a partner in Singapore to re-route trade and banking facilities over a certain percentage of commission. If importation are to done, re-routing instructions regarding Principal’s invoice are to be sent to our partnered company in Singapore D/A method is used to trade between Singapore and Myanmar since the volume of payment and trading goods are big enough of not worth any discrepancies related risk.
